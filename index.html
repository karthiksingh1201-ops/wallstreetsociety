About

Why we exist

Wall Street Society is a student-led finance club dedicated to building real-world market knowledge through research, debate, and community.

  • Original research papers on Indian and global markets
  • Weekly market discussion sessions and stock pitches
  • Guest lectures from industry professionals
  • Case competitions and portfolio simulations
  • A network of finance-minded students across batches

"The stock market is a device for transferring money from the impatient to the patient."

— Warren Buffett
